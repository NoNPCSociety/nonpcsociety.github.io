Founder Reward: 15% of total supply, locked for 6 months.
After the lock period ends, tokens are released linearly over the following 6 months (~16.6% per month), a schedule designed to support market stability and avoid sudden unlocks.
The 15% DAO Treasury allocation is reserved for community‑governed initiatives as the project moves toward greater decentralization.   

Fair-Style, Community-First Presale: Our presale is designed to be as close as possible to a fair launch while still funding the initial operations needed to grow the project.

  • 85% of all SOL raised in the presale will be added to the Raydium liquidity pool, aiming to provide deep liquidity and help absorb volatility, though it cannot eliminate market risk.
  • The remaining 15% is allocated to the founder for initial operational and development costs, separate from the 5% Development Fund reserved in tokenomics.
  • No private round, VC allocation, or discounted seed investors — there are no hidden bags waiting to dump on the market.

This structure keeps $NONPC as a community-first, transparent launch where sell pressure from large early investors is intentionally limited.

GOVERNANCE & VOTING MECHANISMS

In pursuit of a true DAO, $NONPC governance is expected to initially adopt token‑weighted voting — each token equals one unit of voting power. As with all roadmap items, this model and any future alternatives are subject to technical feasibility and community approval.

  • Quadratic Voting
    voting power grows with the square root of holdings, reducing whale dominance.
  • Delegated Voting
    holders can delegate to trusted representatives to raise participation.
  • Identity or SBT‑based Voting (experimental)
    future options for one‑person‑one‑vote using decentralized identity.

The community will decide which model(s) to adopt during Phase 3: The Uprising and beyond.
